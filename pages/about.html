What is the purpose of this website?

There is no specific reason I made this website, or why it is the way it is. It started off with my quick obsession with chasing nostalgia in older technology. As I was researching CRT screens, wii, ps1's and all sorts of other stuff. I was researching what to do with these monitors and made a list. This list included making a 1.0 HTML inspired website, a few weeks later here we are with whatever this is :O

As I was starting out on this website, I was basing it off of an idea for a game I want to make. This resulted in the table and monitor on the main page. After that I just cranked out a bunch of things that I wanted to make, or stuff that I impulsively found hilarious.
